About me

I'm awholegnuworld. I am a programmer and a musician who enjoys designing privacy-centric tools. Currently, I am in my sophomore year of high school. Why did I pick my username? I found it funny. I used to go by Goman60, and still do on some older accounts. I really like GNU and support the Free Software movement, hence my choice in name. I am not affiliated with GNU in any way (sadly). I license all of my work under the GPLv3 when possible, and I try to only incorporate GPL-ed software in my programs that I make.

In my free time, I like to read and play my trumpet. I am in marching band and jazz band at my school, and enjoy being in both. When I read, I often read fantasy novels, or mystery novels, but mainly religious books. I am a Christian and I am always trying to find some way to serve the Lord through programming.

Projects

Pinglist

One of my oldest FOSS projects, I don't really update this one much. It is licensed as GPLv3 and free for anyone to use. Funnily enough, I didn't actually intend to publish this project. I was working on a different (now defunct) project and had to design pinglist as a component of that. Pinglist allows you to read from a text file and ping each domain/IP in it, while still having most of the options from the ping command.
